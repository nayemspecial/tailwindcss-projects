Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our web development services.

How long does a project take?
Timelines vary. A corporate site takes 2-4 weeks, while a custom SaaS platform can take 8-12 weeks depending on complexity.
Do you offer support?
Yes! We provide 1 month of free support with every project to ensure smooth operation. Extended maintenance packages are available.
What is your payment structure?
Typically, we work on a 40% upfront, 30% milestone, and 30% on completion basis. We are flexible for larger projects.
Which technologies do you use?
We specialize in the PHP ecosystem (Laravel, WordPress) and JavaScript ecosystem (Vue.js, React.js, Node.js) along with MySQL databases.
Can you update my existing site?
Absolutely. We can audit your current website, optimize code, improve speed, or completely redesign it with modern tech.
Will my site be mobile-friendly?
Yes, 100%. All our websites are developed with a "mobile-first" approach using responsive frameworks like Tailwind CSS.
