Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working at Projukti Plus

What is the work culture like at Projukti Plus?

We foster a collaborative, innovative, and inclusive work environment. Our culture emphasizes continuous learning, work-life balance, and open communication. We believe in empowering our team members to take ownership of their work while providing support and mentorship.

Do you offer remote work options?

Yes! We offer flexible work arrangements including full remote, hybrid, and office-based options. Many of our team members work remotely from different locations. We provide all necessary equipment and support for remote work, including high-speed internet allowance and home office setup support.

What growth opportunities are available?

We're committed to your professional growth. Opportunities include: regular training programs, conference sponsorships, certification courses, mentorship from senior developers, clear career progression paths, leadership training, and the chance to work on diverse projects across different industries.

What is the interview process timeline?

Typically, the entire process takes 2-3 weeks from application to offer: Application Review (1-2 days) → Initial Screening (3-5 days) → Technical Assessment (5-7 days) → Team Interview (7-10 days) → Final Interview (10-14 days) → Offer (14-21 days). We strive to keep candidates informed at every stage.

Do you hire fresh graduates?

Absolutely! We have an internship program and entry-level positions for recent graduates. We look for passion, willingness to learn, and problem-solving skills. Our structured onboarding and mentorship program helps new graduates transition smoothly into professional roles.
