Our Journey

From a small startup to a leading web development agency in Bangladesh

July 2020

The Beginning

Founded by Md. Nayemur Rahman with a vision to create exceptional web solutions. Started as a solo freelancer working from a small office in Khulna.

March 2021

First Major Project

Landed our first enterprise client - a Laravel-based inventory management system. Successfully delivered ahead of schedule with exceptional quality.

September 2021

Team Expansion

Grew from a solo operation to a team of 5 talented developers and designers. Moved to our first proper office space in Khulna city.

January 2022

SaaS Specialization

Launched our SaaS development division. Started working with international clients from USA, UK, and Australia on complex SaaS platforms.

June 2023

PlasicSaaS Brand Launch

Introduced our premium brand "PlasicSaaS" focused on enterprise-grade web applications. Expanded team to 15+ members with specialized roles.

Present

Industry Recognition

Recognized as one of the leading web development agencies in Bangladesh. Working with 50+ clients globally across various industries.
